Studify.

A Mobile Quiz Application for Children

Studify is mobile quiz application designed to help children improve their knowledge and skills in various subjects such as English, Math, and Science. The app is specifically tailored for preschool up to third grade students, making it an ideal tool for parents and teachers to supplement their child's education.

it was my first attempt in UI Design.

About the UI

To make Studify engaging and fun for children, I incorporated bright and vibrant colors into the application's design. I also made sure to include cute and friendly characters that will appeal to young children.
